About The Secure Planet

Welcome to The Secure Planet. We are a premier information security, cyber audit compliance, digital forensics, and professional security consulting publication.

Our Mission

Our core mission is to empower cybersecurity professionals, auditors, system architects, and researchers with pragmatic, actionable guidance on vulnerability assessment, compliance frameworks (CISSP, CISA, CISM, ISO 27001), digital forensics tools, and cloud infrastructure security.

Core Focus Areas

  • Information Security & Governance: Best practices for ISO/IEC standards, zero-trust architecture, and identity management.
  • Audit Compliance: Regulatory auditing frameworks, automated continuous compliance, and risk assessment checklists.
  • Digital Forensics: Image forensic analysis, memory corruption inspection, open-source investigation toolsets (Sherloq, volatility), and malware incident response.
  • Cyber Security Training: Free curated learning tracks, command line tips, and defensive toolkits.
