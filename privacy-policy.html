Privacy Policy

At The Secure Planet (accessible from www.thesecureplanet.com), one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by The Secure Planet and how we use it.

Log Files & Analytics

The Secure Planet follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. All hosting companies do this as a standard part of hosting services analytics. The information collected by log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks.

Cookies and Web Beacons

Like any other website, The Secure Planet uses 'cookies'. These cookies are used to store information including visitors' preferences, and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited. The information is used to optimize the users' experience by customizing our web page content based on visitors' browser type and/or other information.

Security Commitment

We respect your digital privacy and adhere to secure HTTPS communication protocols across all pages.
